Online Job Portal System

Job Portal

As a Front-End Angular Developer, I developed the frontend of an online job portal using Angular. The platform enabled Job Seekers to register, manage their profiles, search for jobs, and submit applications, providing a seamless user experience for job hunting. The online job portal frontend has three main modules:

  • Job Seeker (for registration, profile management, job search, and applications)
  • Job Provider (for posting jobs and managing applicants)
  • Platform Admin (for overseeing platform-wide operations and user management)

Job Providers, I implemented features that allowed them to create and manage job listings, review candidate applications, and communicate with potential hires directly through the portal. This helped streamline the recruitment process for businesses.

I built a Platform Admin dashboard for managing user roles, moderating job postings, and monitoring platform activity. The system was built using Angular, TypeScript, HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap, and RxJS to ensure it was responsive, scalable, and user-friendly
